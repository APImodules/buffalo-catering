STUDIO

The OBO&HOBOS Recording Studio is a creative space built for artists who aim for uncompromising sound. Located in Moscow, our facility offers a full-cycle music production process — from recording and mixing, to professional mastering and distribution. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and driven by experienced sound engineers, our studio has become a hub for electronic music creators who perform across Russia and internationally.

Whether you are producing your debut single or completing a full-length album, OBO&HOBOS Studio provides the tools, expertise, and atmosphere to transform ideas into releases heard worldwide.
