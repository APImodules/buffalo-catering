About OBO&HOBOS Label

OBO&HOBOS is a dynamic electronic music label based in Moscow, dedicated to promoting innovative deep house, techno, and experimental sounds. Our mission is to support talented artists and deliver premium-quality music experiences worldwide.

Our label offers comprehensive artist development including:

  • Professional recording and production support
  • Marketing and promotion tailored to electronic music audiences
  • Event organization and community engagement
  • Distribution through major digital platforms and physical releases
  • Collaborations with visual artists for immersive live experiences

We pride ourselves on fostering a creative family atmosphere, combining artistic freedom with industry professionalism to help our roster achieve long-term success.
