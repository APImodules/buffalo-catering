OBO&HOBOS RECORD LABEL

For over 15 years, OBO&HOBOS has been uniting and empowering artists in the electronic music scene. Our signature sound is crafted by a dedicated team of composers, producers, and session musicians. Artists on our label are recognized both in Russia and internationally, consistently drawing attention with their unique performances and innovative projects.

Our state-of-the-art studio facilities provide a full production cycle: from initial recording, mixing, and mastering, to wide-scale distribution, media support, and live event management. OBO&HOBOS is where music ideas turn into global experiences.
